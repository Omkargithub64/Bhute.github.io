B a m b o o

Bamboo belongs to the Bambusoideae subfamily of the perennial evergreen grass family Poaceae .

They are woody or herbaceous plants which are found diversified in temperate and tropical Asia, Africa, etc.

Bamboos are one of the fastest growing plants in the world. Certain species of bamboo can grow 910 mm (36 inch) within 24-hour, at a rate of almost 40 mm an hour (a growth around 1 mm every 90 seconds).Giant bamboos are the largest members of the grass family. This rapid growth and tolerance for marginal land, make bamboo a good candidate for afforestation, carbon sequestration and climate change mitigation.
